Can I make it my profession?

Yes, you can. But how? You need to start off by getting famous. You have already won quite a lot of local competitions, that's good. That has done half of your job for you already. Now all you have to do is aim big and attend bigger competitions on a national level and get recognized. Now that you have a recognition, you've to prove your mettle by showing your talent to the people forming a national team with plenty of sponsors in their pockets. And Boom! You are now part of a team which represents your country and if you win the International tournaments, conducted by various organizations like the E-Sports League (ESL), your team wins $250,000 which is a HUGE amount of money. You are now a successful professional.
